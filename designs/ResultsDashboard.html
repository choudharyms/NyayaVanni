Plain language summary

This is a standard residential lease agreement for a duration of 11 months. It establishes that you are the tenant and the other party is the owner of the property located in Bangalore. The document outlines your right to occupy the premises in exchange for a monthly payment called 'rent'.

Importantly, it confirms your security deposit amount and the conditions under which it will be returned. The agreement also specifies who is responsible for utility bills and minor repairs. In simple terms, it's a "live-and-pay" contract that protects both you and the landlord from sudden changes in terms.
